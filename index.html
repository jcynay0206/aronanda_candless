Our Story

JONARA isn't just
a brand — it's a
name made of love.

Two kids who are the reason behind every product we make.

JONARA Beauty is a 100% vegan, handcrafted cosmetics brand created for the Latina woman in the United States. We make lip colors, skincare, and beauty products that speak your language — literally.

Formulated with natural, plant-based ingredients, inspired by the flavors and botanicals of Latin America, and made in small batches with the same care you put into everything you love.

We don't just make beauty products. We make beauty products that feel like they were made for you — because they were.

Behind the Gloss

Made with intention

Every Jonara gloss is handcrafted in small batches — no shortcuts, no fillers. Just clean ingredients, real color, and care in every step.

🌿

Plant-Based Ingredients

We source only vegan, cruelty-free ingredients — nourishing oils, natural waxes, and botanical pigments that care for your lips.

⚗️

Small Batch Mixing

Each formula is mixed by hand in small quantities to ensure consistency, freshness, and the perfect texture every time.
